### The Flame &nbsp;`latest`

> A reflective practice app for iOS and web — helping people develop a deeper relationship with their own mind.

I took this from concept to App Store as a **solo founder**: full-stack development, iOS packaging, payment infrastructure, and go-to-market. The Flame is built around **49 concepts** — words like *courage*, *acceptance*, and *equanimity* — each paired with an essay, reflection prompt, real-world challenge, and check-in. A living flame metaphor responds to your depth of engagement over time.
